**Complement to redact, not a competitor.** redact *deletes* content and removes you from data brokers; we're the **targeting brain** that says *what* to remove — specifically the non-obvious items keyword filters miss — and proves anonymization alone doesn't stop inference. Natural handoff: **we flag → redact (or the user) removes.**

**Opposite of Whitebridge.ai.** Whitebridge profiles *other* people for background checks (aggregates public data on a target). We are **self-audit** — the same capability, inverted ethics — with a **benchmark** and a **defense loop** it has no reason to build.

**No direct competitor** does the self-audit **benchmark + proven-defense** loop (currency-checked 2026 — no new entrant in this lane).

**What we're not:** a deletion tool, a people-search/OSINT tool, or a social-media manager.

**The wedge:** the proven before/after (*0.86 → 0.21*) plus a credible SynthPAI number — a feature-shaped **"hire me" artifact** aimed squarely at redact.dev.
