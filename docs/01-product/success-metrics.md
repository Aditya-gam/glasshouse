## Technical (provable) — measured per `measure/*`
- **Text** top-1 / top-3 on **SynthPAI** — establish a baseline on the first clean run, then improve; a **CI floor** prevents regression (no hard target before the baseline exists).
- **Image** accuracy on **VIP + own labeled photos** — supplementary rigor; reported with humble intervals, not overclaimed.
- **Defense effectiveness** — median **proven** confidence drop on targeted attributes (independent adversary, with intervals + the value-recovery flip), not a self-reported number.
- **Latency + cost per audit** — captured to `run_metrics` (the optimize loop).

## Project (the real point)
- A **live URL** a recruiter/redact can click.
- A written **self-audit case study** — a real before/after on the builder's own footprint.
- The **redact outreach** artifact (the feature-shaped "hire me").

The technical numbers exist to *back* the project outcomes — the live demo and the case study are the actual goal.
