**v1 is not a revenue product — it's a portfolio piece and a targeted "hire me" artifact** aimed at redact.dev. Distribution = the **live URL** + the **self-audit case study** + direct outreach. Dogfood on the builder's own footprint; lead with the SynthPAI number and a before/after.

## Plausible future model (noted, not built in v1)
- **Free** self-audit on your own data — the wedge and the trust-builder.
- **Paid** continuous monitoring / alerting on new exposure.
- **Org / exec tier** for multi-subject (exec/brand) protection — maps to redact's exec-protection product.

We deliberately **don't monetize v1**: building freemium plumbing would distract from the proof-of-capability that is the actual goal. The architecture (multi-tenant, RBAC, async) is already shaped for the future tiers, so monetization is a later switch, not a rebuild.
